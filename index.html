Projects

Smashing Sadness

Smashing Sadness is a VR experience that helps people to release their negative emotions by playing alone with music and interact with memories from the scene "Kitchen". In the project, an unique mechanism of recording and playing back sounds with corresponding actions (Loops) is introduced for users to generate their own music. Developed over two months by a team of three using Unity, I not only made key decisions shaping the project's direction but also contributed to the development of spatialization of sounds and logic implementation.

Augmented Gruffalo

Augmented Gruffalo brought the story of "The Gruffalo" to life using an augmented reality (AR) app. This AR app illustrate the story of the classic children book "The Gruffalo" by scanning the physical book and using virtual objects. The storyline covers the mouse encountering the fox, the owl, the snake, and finally the Gruffalo, culminating in the mouse being eaten by Gruffalo. Developed over two mounths by using AR Foundation and Vuforia on Unity, I contributed the encounter of Gruffalo in the project, with skills of AR tracking, raycasting, photogrammetry, 3D UI and animations.
