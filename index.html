Smashing Sadness

Smashing Sadness is a VR experience that helps people to release their negative emotions by playing alone with music and interact with memories from the scene "Kitchen". In the project, an unique mechanism of recording and playing back sounds with corresponding actions (Loops) is introduced for users to generate their own music. The project is developed in 3 months.
