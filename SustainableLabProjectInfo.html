Sustainable Material Lab - Automobile

The Sustainable Material Lab is an immersive educational platform under development, designed for young automotive designers to explore and understand the applications of environmentally friendly materials in vehicle manufacturing. Utilizing VR and AR technologies, the project offers three interactive scenarios: a World Map that compares global automotive recycling policies, a Recycling Plant where users engage with eco-friendly material processes, and a Material Lab that demonstrates the environmental impact of material choices in real-time. The platform aims to enhance designers' knowledge of sustainable practices, foster environmental awareness, and provide a compelling, interactive learning experience. Currently in development, the project continues to refine its educational content and user interactions, with plans to integrate real-time carbon footprint analysis and expand its scope in the future.
